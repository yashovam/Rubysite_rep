Welcome to our exclusive store. We have a unique product, real natural gems mined over forty years ago, Never burned to enhance color, as mining operations do today. These Gems are absolutely, unadulterated, pure, extremely rare Rubies. They are from the private collection of an Italian diamantaire, a Monsieur Benefico. Therefore, proudly in memory of our father, who used to bow and kiss the hands of ladies from Milan to Montecarlo, Sotheby to Christies... just to value the gemstones on their fingers. But being an extremely distinguished Gentleman and quite good looking, said ladies could not distinguish his love of Gems from their own charms. Please enjoy our wide range of colors and natural inclusions.

Rubies are commonly found in 1 carat sizes. Stones that weigh over 2 carats are considerably rarer and are prized much higher than sapphires of equal size. Rubies of over 3 carats, such as this exceptional specimen in our store are extremely rare. These are considered far rarer than diamonds of the same weight
